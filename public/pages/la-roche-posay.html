The Problem

When Skin Becomes Dry and Uncomfortable

Skin irritation and dryness are more common than most people think — and they often have nothing to do with your skincare routine. The causes are usually external:

Common causes:
Weather changes and cold air
Frequent hand washing
Skin sensitivity or reactions
Environmental stress
This can lead to:
Flaking and peeling
Redness and irritation
Tightness and discomfort
Slow skin recovery
Why It Happens

It's Often a Skin Barrier Issue

Your skin has a natural protective barrier that keeps moisture in and irritants out. When it becomes weakened, everything gets harder.

💧
Skin loses hydration faster — moisture escapes instead of staying locked in where it belongs.
🔴
Irritation increases — with the barrier weakened, external triggers cause more visible reactions.
⏳
Recovery slows down — skin that can't retain moisture takes longer to heal and rebalance itself.
